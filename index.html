What we offer

Specialist orthodontic care for every age and every case

Whether your child needs early interceptive care, your teenager wants Invisalign, or you are an adult considering braces for the first time, every treatment at Wilke Orthodontics is planned and supervised by Dr. Wilke himself.

Invisalign

Clear aligner therapy for adults and adolescents. Removable, virtually invisible, and planned by a board-eligible orthodontic specialist so your results are precise from the first tray to the last.

Adults + Teens

Invisalign Teen

The same clear aligner technology as Invisalign, designed for growing patients. Compliance indicators, extra aligners for the unexpected, and space built in for teeth still erupting.

Teenagers

Braces

Metal, clear, and self-ligating bracket systems for the full range of cases. Proven technology, expertly placed, and the right choice when precision and predictability matter most.

Children, Teens + Adults

SureSmile 3D Scanning

No more alginate impressions. Our SureSmile 3D digital workflow captures your bite in full detail in minutes, letting Dr. Wilke plan your treatment on a precise digital model before a single wire is placed.

All Patients

Early (Phase 1) Treatment

Some growth patterns respond best to early intervention. Dr. Wilke will let you know honestly whether your child benefits from Phase 1 care now, or whether waiting is the right call.

Ages 7 to 11

Free Consultation

Your first visit at any of our three Northeast Wisconsin offices costs you nothing. We will take a look, talk through your options, and give you an honest picture of what treatment involves before you commit to anything.

Meet Dr. Wilke

Thirty-one years of specialist orthodontic care, right here in Wisconsin

Dr. Kevin J. Wilke, DDS, MS (Orthodontics)

Dr. Kevin Wilke grew up in Gillett, Wisconsin, earned his Doctor of Dental Surgery at Marquette University in 1993, and completed his Master of Science in Orthodontics there in 1995. He came straight home to Northeast Wisconsin and has been here ever since.

In thirty-one years he has built three permanent offices across Brown, Door, and Oconto counties. Wilke Orthodontics is independent and doctor-owned. It is not a DSO. The care your family receives comes from one orthodontist and the team built around him over three decades.

He lives in the region with his wife Mary and their three children. He knows these communities the way a neighbor does.

Three offices, one specialist

We come to your corner of Northeast Wisconsin

Most orthodontic practices that claim a region run a single office. Wilke Orthodontics operates three permanent locations in Brown, Door, and Oconto counties so specialist care is closer to where you live.

Three towns. Thirty-one years.

Wilke Orthodontics started in Green Bay in 1995. Over the years Dr. Wilke added the Sturgeon Bay office to serve Door County families and the Oconto Falls office to bring specialist care to Oconto County without a long drive.

All three offices provide the same treatments and the same level of care. When you need to move between locations for any reason, your records travel with you.
